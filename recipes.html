Pork Rib Soup

  • 1 chinese radish or winter melon half
  • 2 lb of pork spare ribs
  • 1 carrot (optional)
  • ginger root
  • 1 yellow onion
  • green onion
  • white pepper and salt to taste
  1. 1. start by blanching the pork ribs with 2-4 slices of ginger and 1 ring of yellow onion for 10 minutes.
  2. 2. meanwhile, cut carrot and radish into large chunks of similar size. dice the remaining yellow onion and slice the ginger.
  3. 3. transfer the meat only into another boiling pot of water. dispose of the water the meat cooked in previously. this is to cleanse the meat of the blood.
  4. 4. add diced yellow onion and ginger. lower heat to a low boil and allow meat to cook for 30 minutes.
  5. 5. after 30 minutes, add vegetables and lower heat to a simmer. allow to simmer for 1.5-2 hours.
  6. 6. season with white pepper and salt to taste. garnish with green onions and enjoy!

Soft Boiled Soy Eggs

  • eggs
  • soy sauce
  • water
  • sugar
  • onion
  • chinese five spice
  • 1 clove garlic
  • sesame oil
  • sesame seeds
  1. 1. fill a pan just until the water goes up about an inch. boil your eggs in this with the cover on for 6-8 minutes. you can add vinegar if you would like, since it helps with peeling.
  2. 2. take the eggs out and leave them in cold water.
  3. 3. to make the marinade, use a 1:1 ratio of soysauce and water. add chopped onions and minced garlic. add 2 tablespoons of sugar, a splash of sesame oil, and chinese five spice to taste. sprinkle with sesame seeds.
  4. 4. peel your eggs carefully and submerge them in the marinade. soak overnight, then enjoy!

Shrimp and Napa

  • shrimp
  • napa cabbage
  • 1 egg
  • ginger root
  • 1 clove garlic
  • green onion
  • chicken broth
  • white rice (optional)
  1. 1. start by putting the white rice into your rice cooker and turning it on.
  2. 2. meanwhile, defrost your shrimp and chop up your napa cabbage. mince your garlic and ginger.
  3. 3. add ginger and garlic and oil to a wok. cook until fragrant, then add the shrimp and napa. once the shrimp starts turning pink, add chicken broth and stir fry an egg into the mixture.
  4. 4. season with salt and pepper to taste. top with green onions and serve on rice. enjoy!

Gochujang Tofu Soup

  • tofu
  • bok choy
  • 1 egg (optional)
  • gochujang
  • rice cakes or dduk
  • yellow onion
  • green onion
  • shrimp (optional)
  1. 1. start by boiling water.
  2. 2. meanwhile, defrost your shrimp and chop up your bok choy and onions.
  3. 3. stir a spoonful of gochujang into your water. add shrimp, tofu, onions, and bok choy with salt and red chili pepper to taste.
  4. 4. add in your rice cakes or dduk once the shrimp starts turning pink.
  5. 5. once finished, turn off the heat and crack in an egg. enjoy on its own or with white rice!

Bibimbop

  • 1 egg
  • protein (marinated beef, pork, chicken, or tofu) (optional)
  • 1 egg (optional)
  • gochujang
  • carrots
  • mushrooms
  • green onion
  • spinach
  • white rice
  • sesame oil
  • sesame seeds
  • cucumber
  • beansprouts
  1. 1. start by cooking your white rice in a rice cooker.
  2. 2. meanwhile, take your pre-marinated protein out and cook it with sesame oil.
  3. 3. cut your mushrooms into slices. cut your carrots into thin sticks. cut your cucumbers into half moons.
  4. 4. in a second pan, cook each vegetable on its own in sesame oil, except for the cucumbers. then fry an egg.
  5. 5. get a bowl of white rice and top it with your protein and vegetables. put a scoop of gochujang on top and then add your egg. sprinkle with sesame seeds and green onions. then break your egg and mix together to enjoy!
