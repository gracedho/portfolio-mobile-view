About Me

hi! i’m grace ho. i’m a student at the university of michigan studying information science.

since i’ve started college, i’ve grown more and more interested in cooking and in food. i truly believe that a home cooked meal is a type of magic on its own that shows love. because of that, my new year’s resolution for 2022 was to cook more! in addition to that, i’ve also been exploring more, traveling more, and trying new foods. while i am based in ann arbor, i have been traveling to new york city and san francisco recently. my favorite place to travel so far has been taipei, taiwan, and it was definitely the most delicious trip too! when i was younger, i used to bake more, but recently, i have been cooking a lot more, so follow my journey in learning about food!
